Jan's story

How did we get here

Jan started practising commercial website development in 2007. In 2010, he and two other partners estabilished Kurzor. Kurzor originally started as a web development company, serving happy customers and obtaining great reviews especially for company websites and custom-developed web portals. In later years, it focused on outsourcing local developers into customer teams and projects, namely in the UK, US and European markets. Especially in this period people in Kurzor gained the most experience, having been through the most difficult challenges and demanding clients. The company was peacefully disbanded from 2023 and Jan continues in his own brand, invenus, supporting many of the original customers and clients from Kurzor.
